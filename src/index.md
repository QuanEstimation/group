# **QuanEstimation Group**

Hello! Welcome to the home page of QuanEstimation group. We are a theoretical group located at the Center for Theoretical Physics and School of Physics and Optoelectronic Engineering, 
Hainan University. We focus on the studies in the field of quantum technology, quantum information theory, and fundations of quantum mechanics. 

## **Opening positions**

Master students, Ph.D. students, and postdoctors are welcome! Please contact Prof. Jing Liu (jing.liu@hainanu.edu.cn) for more details.
